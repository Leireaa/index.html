Who We Are

Welcome to Mikhylerayne’s Street Foodies — your local home for sweet and savory delights. What started as a small passion for homemade desserts and snacks grew into a business that celebrates flavor, creativity, and Filipino comfort food.

Our Story

Our journey began with a simple goal: to share happiness through every bite. We take pride in offering food that’s not just delicious but made with care and genuine love for our customers. From street favorites to comforting desserts, we bring a taste of joy to every table.

Mission

To provide quality, affordable, and heartfelt food that makes every customer feel at home — wherever they are.

Values

  • Quality and Freshness in Every Bite
  • Honest and Friendly Service
  • Love for Local Flavor
